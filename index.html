UBC Nion is a web-based tool for calculating inelastic mean free path (IMFP) and knock-on damage threshold for electron microscopy. The IMFP calculator codes were modified from Dr. A. Husain's codes, which were based on the Matlab code of Dr. R. Egerton.

Webpage maintained by Sean Kung at the University of British Columbia.
